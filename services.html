Services

Time is so precious and for many people the last thing you want to do when you do have free time, is housework. Don’t worry, we got this. So put your feet up, relax; order in, or go out; put a movie on, take the kids out or organise that date night; because housework is a thing of the past.

We also work with business to keep their company premises spick and span. From restaurants, to office buildings, garages to shop floors; we are trusted to attend regularly to take the head-ache out of company housekeeping.



The time your cleaner is at your property is for you and is happy to do any other tasks in the time agreed. From general cleaning, tidying, ironing, laundry etc to other tasks such as pet care and shopping. De-cluttering to be arranged separately.

We will work with you to create a cleaning agreement that is personalised and perfect for your home, business, family and commitments.

You can trust that everyone at Mint Leaf Clean is DBS certified, all housekeepers have gone through the extensive Mint Leaf Training program, are uniformed when working, will sign a confidentiality agreement and are fully insured. All our products are Eco friendly; we bring our own equipment and are trained in health and safety at home and in the work place.
