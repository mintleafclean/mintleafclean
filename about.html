About Us


I’ll tell you a secret... we’ve found our niche, what really makes us tick... and that is, making lives easier.



Our founder Kristi Juliussen started Mint Leaf Clean after years of working and training others to an exceptional standard in the luxury hospitality industry, and elite private households in the UK and abroad. She set out to create a service which would change her clients lives for the better, giving them more time and freedom. The aim was to make home-life easier while bringing high end quality to home and businesses.. She did this by sourcing a dedicated team of specialists who are trained in all aspects of housekeeping, to offer a thorough housekeeping service.

We are passionate about beautiful, healthy spaces because we know that our surroundings directly effect our mood. Clean and tidy spaces induce a feeling of wellness, clearing the mind and freeing time. Our guiding principles are to do work in a way that impacts everyone our business touches, including the community and environment, in a positive way. To lead by our values and keep our clients at the heart of what we do.

We’re leaders in the field with over 15 years experience. But we don’t like to brag, we are proud of what we do and bringing ongoing exceptional value to our clients is why we push forward.

So how do we do this?


We make every effort to be an ethical company, we use eco-friendly products and have our environmental impact in mind throughout, we strive to work with charities and in the community, we look after our staff with continued training and support, we have a thirst of knowledge and stay up to date with new systems and techniques, with the aim to make our clients lives easier.

The boring bit – we are fully insured; our staff are DBS checked and we are always uniformed when working. We use all our own equipment and use products that are safe for your home, family and pets.
